Project: Gismo - Multi-Sensor Robot

Detailed description of the Gismo robot project, its purpose, the build process, components used, programming involved, and its capabilities.

Gismo is a personal robotics project born from a desire to create a versatile and intelligent mobile platform. The goal was to integrate a variety of sensors and actuators to enable autonomous navigation, environmental interaction, and data collection.

Key Components & Integration

  • Controller: Raspberry Pi Zero 2W
  • Motor Control: L298N Motor Drivers
  • Servo Control: PCA9685 Servo Driver
  • Display: SSD1306 OLED Display
  • Inertial Measurement: MPU9250 IMU
  • Vision: Pi Camera
  • Sensors: Edge detection, IR proximity, touch sensors, ultrasonic distance sensors, sound sensor.
