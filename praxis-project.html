Project: Praxis - Self-Evolving AI System

Praxis represents an ambitious exploration into creating an AI architecture that can learn, adapt, and evolve autonomously. The core idea is to build a system that doesn't just execute predefined tasks but can actively monitor its own performance, identify inefficiencies, and dynamically restructure itself to improve.

Key Features & Concepts

  • Modular Architecture (MetaAgent, TaskAgents, SkillAgents)
  • Bio-inspired Emergent Intelligence
  • Iterative Evolution & Mutation Engine
  • Fitness Evaluation and Reinforcement Learning Principles
  • Dynamic KnowledgeBase and FactMemory
